About Us

BusInfo is a web platform created by a group of engineering student.BusInfo is an agency which helps bus travel agencies to collect and store ther data on a single platform (website), in a properly arranged structure and use the data in an efficient way.

Tracking of Vehicles owned

We keep track of the vehicles owned by the Bus travel agency, from the date of vehicle registered to various other details such as Permit of the vehicle, insurance of the vehicle and the very basic details of the vehicle such as PUC etc.

Tracking the details of drivers

We keep the track of each every driver registered with the company. Keeping the track of the driver's license and notifying the owner of the bus travel agency as the daet of expiry of the license gets nearer so that the owner of the Bus travel agency can ask the driver to renew their license.

Our future plans

We don't want to be limited as managing website. We would like to make some good and impactul changes in our existing system. We would like to create meaningful insights regarding the travel quality. We would also like to make our road travel safer for ourselves and aswell as others.

We plan to track the vehicles used by the agency. This would help to upgrade the service by providing the required quantity of vehicles and drivers in the area of demand.

We also plan to keep the track of the distance and time travelled by a particular dirver so that he doesnt get exhausted and may get any heakth issues which may lead to any kind of accidents.
